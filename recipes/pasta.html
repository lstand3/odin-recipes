Recipe

Creamy cheesecake with graham cracker crust.

This cheesecake is magical and delicious with a creamy base and a sour cream top layer. The graham cracker crust makes this cake even more scrumptious and perfectly balances the creamy flavor./p>

You will need basic kitchen equipment and ingredients like eggs, creamcheese, vanilla extract, and graham crackers. See the list below for all ingredients needed.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Creamcheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Graham Cracker

Steps

  1. Prepare workspace.
  2. Mix creamcheese.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Bake at 350 for 20 mins.
  5. Add sour cream.
  6. Put in graham cracker crust.
